# Design Brief — M M Detalhe Automóvel

## Visual Direction

**Mood**: Premium, sleek, luxury — think high-end car brand meets boutique service
**Tone**: Professional but approachable. Confident craftsmanship.

## Color Palette

- **Primary**: Deep black / charcoal (`#0a0a0a`, `#1a1a1a`)
- **Accent**: Gold / amber (`#d4a853`) — luxury feel, used sparingly for CTAs and highlights
- **Secondary**: Cool silver/slate (`#94a3b8`) — for body text on dark backgrounds
- **White**: For contrast sections and text on dark backgrounds
- **Gradient**: Subtle dark-to-darker gradients for depth

## Typography Direction

- **Headings**: Bold, uppercase, wide tracking — commanding presence
- **Body**: Clean, modern sans-serif — easy to read on dark backgrounds
- **Numbers/Stats**: Large, bold, gold accent

## Layout Principles

- Dark backgrounds dominate — the cars are the color
- Large imagery — hero should be full-viewport with dramatic lighting
- Before/after slider is the centerpiece — dramatic transformations
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Service packages presented as premium tiers (think SaaS pricing cards but automotive)

## Section-Specific Direction

### Hero
- Full-viewport dark background
- Dramatic car image with studio lighting feel
- Gold accent on CTA buttons
- Stats ticker at bottom (cars detailed, years, rating)

### Before/After
- Dark section, slider items centered
- Gold divider line on the slider
- Captions below each comparison

### Services
- Icon cards on dark background
- Subtle border/glow effect on hover
- Gold icon accents

### Pricing
- 3 tiers: Basic, Premium, Ceramic
- Popular tier highlighted with gold border
- Feature checkmarks in gold

### Gallery
- Full-bleed horizontal scroll
- Moody, well-lit car photography style

## Imagery Notes

- All car photos should have dramatic, studio-quality lighting
- Dark backgrounds preferred (garage/workshop aesthetic)
- Close-up detail shots for services (paint reflection, interior leather)
- Before/after pairs should be dramatic transformations
